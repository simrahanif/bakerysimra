About Our Bakery

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by pastry chef Maria Rodriguez, The Hidden Cup Bakery began as a small home-based business with just one oven and a dream. What started as a passion for creating delicious treats for friends and family quickly grew into the beloved neighborhood bakery we are today.

We pride ourselves on using only the finest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible. Every item in our bakery is made from scratch daily, using traditional recipes with a modern twist.

Our Philosophy

At The Hidden Cup, we believe that baking is an art form that should engage all the senses. Our philosophy is simple:

  • Quality ingredients make quality baked goods
  • Every customer should feel like family
  • Traditional methods with innovative flavors
  • Freshness is non-negotiable
  • Sweet memories are made here
